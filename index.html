Privacy Policy

CozyBookHearth — Last updated: April 8, 2026

CozyBookHearth ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a gamified ebook reading application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Summary: We collect minimal data. Your books stay on your device. We don't sell or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Reader Name — An optional display name you choose, visible on leaderboards if you opt in.
  • Privacy Preference — Your chosen privacy tier (public, friends-only, or anonymous).

Information collected automatically:

  • Reading Progress — Page numbers, bookmarks, and highlights for books you import. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Credit Balance — Credits earned from reading and credits purchased. Stored locally and synced to our servers when online.
  • Reading Statistics — Aggregated page counts and session data used for achievements and leaderboards. No content of your books is ever transmitted.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not read, upload, or transmit the content of your books.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or microphone data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell data to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Reading Experience — To save your reading position, bookmarks, highlights, and preferences locally on your device.
  • Credit System — To track credits earned through reading and purchased through in-app purchases.
  • Achievements & Leaderboards — To display your reading milestones and optional leaderboard rankings.
  • Cabin Customization — To save your cabin, background, and cosmetic choices.
  • Purchase Verification — To validate in-app purchases through Google Play.

3. Data Storage & Security

The majority of your data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database. When you create an account and are online, some data (credit balance, achievements, leaderboard rank) is synced to our secure servers powered by Google Firebase.

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication for account security
  • Firestore security rules restricting data access to the owning user
  • Server-side validation of in-app purchase receipts
  • No storage of payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)

4. In-App Purchases

CozyBookHearth offers optional in-app purchases including credit packs and a subscription ("Cozy+"). All purchases are processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. Google's privacy policy governs payment processing.

All in-app purchases are entirely optional. Every item in the shop can be earned for free by reading.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (In-app purchases) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Open Library API (Book cover search) — Used only when you manually search for a book cover. Sends only the book title and author name.

6. Children's Privacy

CozyBookHearth is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — All reading data is stored locally and visible in the app.
  • Delete your data — Uninstalling the app removes all local data. Contact us to request deletion of any server-side data.
  • Control visibility — Use the Privacy settings to control how your name appears on leaderboards (public, friends-only, or fully anonymous).
  • Opt out of leaderboards entirely by choosing the anonymous privacy tier.

8. Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Server-side data (if an account is created) is retained for the life of your account. You may request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
